Tagliafico has been exhibiting since 1971. Individual exhibitions of his work have been held at galleries and institutions such as the Museo Arte Contemporáneo de Caracas (1976); the Alternative Museum, New York (1977); Galerie Dieter-Mueller Roth, Stuttgart (1981); Centro Arte Galeter, Brescia (1991); Museo Alejandro Otero, Caracas (1999). His work has been awarded numerous distinctions including Mention of Honor at the Salon Arturo Michelena, Caracas (1973), Mention of Honor Joan Miró, Barcelona (1974), and the International Association of Art Critics’ International Award (1987).
