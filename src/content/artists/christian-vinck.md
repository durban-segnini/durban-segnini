short_bio: The self-taught Dutch-Venezuelan artist Christian Vinck regards
  himself as belonging to a long tradition of amateur painters. His art explores
  epic themes on a personal scale, reconstructing symbolic memories and historic
  narratives through stories, documents and visual references. Vinck utilizes a
  wide range of source materials – archives, correspondence, found photographs,
  newspaper clippings and articles, notebooks, manuscripts, encyclopedias,
  sticker albums, postcards, and cartographic and digital images – interweaving
  personal narratives with real and fictional histories. Lives and works in
  Madrid.
